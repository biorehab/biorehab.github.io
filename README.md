We are the **Biological Learning and Rehabilitation Group* at the department of [Bioengineering](http://www.bioe-cmcvellore.in/), [Christian Medical College Vellore](http://www.cmch-vellore.edu/).

We are an academic research group interested developing and validating tools and methods for the optimal training and assessment of sensorimotor function following neurological conditions.
