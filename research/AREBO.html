Research / AREBO

AREBO - ARm REhabilitation roBOt is a 2DOF robot for training shoulder functions, in particular the vertical and horizontal flexion/extension. The robot consists of 6DOF with two active DOFs and four passive DOFs, which allows a lot of freedom in where the human sits with respect to the robot when he/she is physically connected to the robot. This makes AREBO very easy to use. AREBO can also be used for training both left and right arms without any change in the robot's kinematic structure.
