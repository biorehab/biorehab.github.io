Research / PLUTO

PLTUO - PLUg and Train rObot is a simple, modular single DOF robot for training a wide range of hand functions. The characteisitic feature of the robot is that it has a single actuator and a set of passive mechanisms that can plugged in and out of the actuator for training different functions.
