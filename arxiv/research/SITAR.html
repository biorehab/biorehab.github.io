Research / SITAR

SITAR - System for Independent Task-Oriented Assessment and Rehabilitation is an interactive upper-limb workstation for training and assess different types of upper-limb movements. The SITAR consists of an eco-system of components that interact with each other to provide multi-modal feedback during training.

The SITAR is being developed in collaboration with The Human Robotics Group at Impeiral College London.
