Research / PNEUBO

PNEUBO - PNEUmatic Planar Arm Rehabilitation RoBOt is a planar arm rehabilitation robot for gravity support arm training PNEUBO is a parallel robot with an RPRPR kinematic structure. The kinematics structure is a triangle where one side is fixed and the lengths of the other two sides can be changed. The prismatic joints are currently implemented using pneumatic cylindrical actuators. The kinematic structure of the robot has been implemented and we are currently in the process of implementing the different control modes for the robot.
