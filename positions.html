Open Positions

We currently have a Senior Research Fellow (SRF) position on a DBT funded project (PLUTO) on developing a hand rehabilitation robot. The following is the advertisement on CMC's weekly news.

The SRF will be responsible for the design, and fabrication of robot mechanisms for assisting different hand movements, along with the associated instrumentation and robot programming for implementing assisted movement therapy. Based on the person's background and interests, there are possibilities for getting involved in some of the other projects at the BRG as well.
